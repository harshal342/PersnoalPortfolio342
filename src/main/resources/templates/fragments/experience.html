Savitribai Phule Pune University

University

Savitribai Phule Pune University, situated in Pune, Maharashtra, India, is one of the country’s most esteemed centers of higher learning. Established in 1949 as the University of Poona, it was later renamed in honor of Savitribai Phule, a visionary social reformer and pioneer of women’s education in the 19th century. Renowned for its academic excellence and vibrant campus life, the university offers a wide spectrum of programs across arts, science, commerce, engineering, and various other disciplines, fostering innovation, research, and holistic development.
